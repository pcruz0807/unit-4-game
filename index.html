The Crystal Collector Game


The Rules


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals to choose from. Each Crystal will a specific amount of points to your total score. When you match your total score to the random number, you win the game. If your total score goes over the ramdom number, game over.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it. A new value to each crystal will change when a new game starts.
